What is DoBeDragon?

DoBeDragon is an internationally acclaimed tabletop game with an expanding card collection, at present totaling 212 one-stars, 104 two-stars, 73 three-stars, 42 four-stars, and 15 five-stars. The sponsored event will feature major creators from the board game industry alongside thematic food and drinks. Come one, come all because DoBeDragon will be hosting a competition that aims to crown the DoBeMaster!

Other activities of note include shopping at select board game shops, participating in the costume contest, getting your face painted, and networking with creators in the industry. At DoBeDragon, we're also interested in supporting aspiring board game designers. By participating in the event, you can meet with the top professionals from our staff and apply for a paid internship.

Awards for participation and winning the competition will be distributed at the close party.
